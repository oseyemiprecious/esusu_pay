Our Best Features

Esusu is not just restricted to thrift collection, we offer a couple of other services

Instant SMS/Email Alerts

The system provides members and management of esusu schemes with instant information on any transction made (SMS/Email Alerts, and paper receipt) guaranteeing security of data and information.

Card Issuance

Esusu Card is made available to customers on demand. With this card, cash withdrawals can be made from different Automated Payment Systems (APS). The use of APS such as ATMs (Automated Teller Machines), POS (Point of Sales) Terminals, Online banking, etc is ideal since the Esusu office is registered with a bank.

Secured Digital Banking

Funds sit on customer's prepaid card, affording them the opportunity to confirm balances at any time. The money is readily available.
